Cabeza Taco - My favorite taco meat!

There is a wide variety of different street foods, but my favorite street food is found in cabeza tacos. Cabeza is the head of a pork, minced to be served in a taco. It is very filling and soft, a great texture and taste. I would go out with highschool friends to different taco stands in my hometown. Maybe the reason why it tastes so good is because I'm reminded of those memories when I eat this dish :)
