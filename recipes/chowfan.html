Chow Fan

Chow Fan - A savory dish that is a staple in many chinese families around the world.

Chow Fan is fried rice, and is found in many cultures around the globe. This particular version of chow fan is Chinese, and features many chinese ingredients. The ingredients being pork intestine, egg, green onion, and much more.

I would have this all the time at my house, there's a funny connection I have to chow fan too actually. When I was a baby first learning to walk, my parents had brought a bowl of chow fan in front of me. I would crawl and crawl, and as they pulled the bowl further I had learned to walk! I caught up to the bowl and had my first memory as a kid :)

  • Pork Intestines
  • Egg
  • Green onion
  • White rice
  • A dash of sesame oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Prep the pan, and add cooked rice.
  2. Fry the rice, and add pork intestine.
  3. Add egg next.
  4. Add green onion.
