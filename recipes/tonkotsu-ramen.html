Tonkotsu Ramen

Tonkotsu Ramen - Who doesn't like ramen?

There is a ramen place called Daikokuya in Little Tokyo, Los Angeles. They sell tonkotsu ramen, and the first time I had it I was blown away. While tonkotsu ramen was never my all time favorite, it is a consistent dish I can always rely on to be good. I remember when I was a kid, I used to go shop around all the japanese stores with my cousins. We'd buy merch from different mangas/animes, and end the day with a nice hot bowl of ramen :)

  • Pork Intestines
  • Soft-boiled eggs
  • Green onions
  • Sesame seeds
  • Bean sproouts
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Nori
  • Bok Choy
  • Ramen
  1. Go to Daikokuya in Little Tokyo, Los Angeles
  2. Be seated, join waitlist if needed
  3. Receive menu
  4. Look at menu
  5. Order Daikokuya Tonkotsu Ramen with Kotteri and salad (salad is to die for)
